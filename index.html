About Me

Let me tell you about myself

I am a first year Creative Digital Media student in ITB. In the summer of 2015 I took part in my Leaving Certificate and then attended Dunboyne College of Further Education to study multimedia for a year. I have a huge interest in the web and also expressing my creative mind. I have developed skills in the following areas- Adobe Illustrator,Adobe Photoshop,Adobe Bridge, Dreamweaver. I also have a keen interest in photography and that can be seen in this portfolio also.
